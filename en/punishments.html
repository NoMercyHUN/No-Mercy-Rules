Punishments

Sanctions depend on the severity and frequency of rule violations.

Sanctions

  • Warning
  • Mute
  • Kick
  • Temporary ban
  • Permanent ban

Instant ban

  • Use of cheats, hacks or external software
  • Intentional use of exploits, bugs or glitches
  • Serious threats or harassment
  • Sharing personal information (doxxing)

Appeal

  • Appeals can be submitted via Discord ticket.
  • Provide your Steam64 ID and the time of the incident.
  • Attach evidence if available.
  • Staff decisions after review are final.
