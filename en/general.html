General Rules

These community rules apply everywhere. On Discord and on the server.

Respect

  • Respect other players and staff members.
  • Insults, racism and hate speech are forbidden.
  • Harassment, threats and intimidation are forbidden.
  • Provoking others and stirring conflicts is forbidden.

Communication

  • Keep a respectful tone.
  • Spam, flooding and intentional disruption are forbidden.
  • Excessive swearing and aggressive communication are forbidden.

Privacy

  • Sharing personal data is forbidden.
  • Doxxing, blackmail and targeted threats are forbidden.

Advertising and content

  • Advertising and self-promotion without staff permission are forbidden.
  • Scams, phishing and malicious links are forbidden.
  • Illegal content is forbidden.

Guidelines

  • Not knowing the rules does not exempt you from consequences.
  • Rules may change.
  • If you have a dispute, open a ticket. Do not handle it in public chat.
