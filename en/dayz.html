Base Building Rules

Building is allowed as long as your base is fair, fully raidable, and does not negatively impact gameplay.

1. Basic Rules

  • Maximum 1 flagpole per group.
  • Your base must remain within a 30 meter radius of the flagpole.

2. Location Restrictions

  • No building near military zones or event areas, or in a way that provides direct line of sight into them.
  • No building on any roads, paths, or tracks where vehicles can pass.
  • No building in police stations, prisons, hospitals, or medical buildings.

3. Structural Requirements

  • Your base must be connected to the ground or to an existing building.
  • "Levitating" bases are not allowed. No more than 1 foundation tile on the perimeter may be floating in the air.
  • Maximum build height is controlled by script (approximately 15 meters from ground level).
  • Your base must be fully raidable by a single player on foot without requiring boosting or dismantling.

4. Doors & Layout

  • Maximum 15 doors per base.
  • Door stacking is not allowed.
  • Doors must be spaced at least one prone player length apart.
  • Two-window airlock systems are not allowed.

5. Prohibited Building Methods

  • Tent stacking is not allowed (placing smaller tents inside larger tents).
  • Trap bases are not allowed.
  • Barbed wire inside your base is not allowed.
  • Any design that forces players into animations is not allowed (jumping, crouching, prone, weapon raised, etc.).

6. Raid Fairness Requirements

  • Your base must not contain any unraidable sections.
  • Tunnels / raid passages must be at least two small walls wide.
