Raid Rules

These rules are designed to keep raiding fair, clear, and enforceable for everyone.

1. Breaching Charge Use

  • Breaching Charges may be used only on doors, hangar doors, garage doors, and gates.
  • A raid is defined as a break-in using a Breaching Charge.

2. Raid Time (C4 / Breaching Charge)

  • Breaching Charge raiding is allowed only during:
  • Friday 18:00–22:00 / Saturday 18:00–22:00 (CET)
  • Using Breaching Charges outside these hours is not allowed.

3. Soft Raiding (Allowed 24/7)

Soft raiding is any base entry method that does not require a Breaching Charge (eco raid). This is allowed at all times, including:

  • Jumping / climbing into a base
  • Climbing or crawling through windows
  • “Soft siding” (dismantling) due to a clear building mistake
  • Using an entrance that was left open

4. No Griefing

  • Griefing is prohibited before, during, and after a raid.
  • What counts as griefing is decided by admins (case-by-case).
  • Examples: unnecessary destruction, making a base unusable, destroying loot on purpose, blocking exits without reason.

5. Building & Rebuild Rules

  • No building during an active raid (this includes placing storage).
  • Rebuilding is allowed only 30 minutes after the last door was destroyed.
  • Tracking the time is the defending team’s responsibility.

6. Logging Rules

  • No logging out inside a base that does not belong to you or your team.
  • No logging out with loot while being raided (combat logging).

7. Prohibited Actions Related to Bases

  • Do not place codelocks on any part of someone else’s base (gates, doors, windows, storage, any element).
  • You may not take over another player’s base (overtake).
  • You may not take over another player’s flagpole.
  • If a base’s flag is completely down (flag fully lowered), the base is considered abandoned and dismantling for materials is allowed.
  • If there is any dispute, open a ticket — an admin will decide.

8. Excessive Dismantling

  • Dismantling bases solely to farm materials is not allowed.
  • Exception: if the base’s flag is completely down (flag fully lowered), the base is considered abandoned and dismantling is allowed.
  • If a base has already been breached and there is a clear path (e.g., open doorways), you must use that path instead of dismantling additional parts.

9. Raid Recording Required

  • You must record your raid(s).
  • Admins may request the video if a rule break is suspected (admin eyes only).
  • Keep your clips for 7 days.

10. Boosting Rules

  • Item boosting is not allowed.
  • Player boosting is allowed with a maximum of 2 players (one on another’s head).
  • Vehicle boosting is allowed.

11. Breaching Charge Amounts (Door Tiers)

  • Tier 1: 2 handmade or 1 heavy
  • Tier 2: 4 handmade or 2 heavy
  • Tier 3: 6 handmade or 3 heavy

12. Illegal Base

  • Raiding a base marked with an “Illegal Base” sign is done at your own risk.
