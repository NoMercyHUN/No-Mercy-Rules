Discord Rules

Channel order, respectful communication and fast support.

Channels

  • Use the appropriate channel for your message.
  • Off-topic content must go to the off-topic channel.
  • Spam, flooding and meaningless message chains are forbidden.

Communication

  • Insults, racism and hate speech are forbidden.
  • Harassment and threats are forbidden.
  • NSFW content is forbidden.

Advertising and links

  • Advertising other servers without staff permission is forbidden.
  • Suspicious links, scams or phishing attempts result in an immediate ban.

Tickets

  • Open a ticket for support requests.
  • Ticket spam or troll tickets are forbidden.
